JUST IN CASE: 4 Types of Cool Cases To Protect Your Device!

Written by Nisa Yaptinchay

ZALORA PH
Follow
Mar 14, 2016 · 3 min read

What’s not to love about modern technology? Apart from ultra-amazing aesthetics, It’s hard to believe how it makes it so easy for us to stay connected to the world. It would seem as though the only feature yet to be realized is for our phones to withstand the force of gravity. Alas, What comes up must come down and our beloved gadgets are no exception.

And while everybody loves the touch and feel of a naked phone (up until it’s dropped, scratched or worse, shattered that is); a cracked iPhone screen can prove to be a total eyesore — or a heart breaker for those OCD with keeping their devices pristine.

In our desperate longing to keep our devices looking like new, the tech Gods have paved way for protective cases in all shapes and sizes, print and designs and more importantly, levels of protection. I for one am a sucker for pretty phone cases while simultaneously believing in the old adage “better safe than sorry”.

Now how to keep your beloved (and very expensive device) stylishly protected? Here are four types of phone cases that are sure to keep your phones looking like new.

1. Tough Cases

For the risk takers and thrill seekers, this is the case for you. Though these cases are quite bulky, it certainly does the tough job of keeping your phones protected from water, dirt, dust and major bumps and bruises.

2. Slim Cases

While we’d love our phone as-is, au naturel in its sleek case-less glory, we wouldn’t dare use it without protection right? To keep your phone safe and slim, Opt for a cute snap case like this by Nisa & Frank. Durable enough to protect against scratches, dents and drops while slim enough to keep your phone looking cute with its vibrant prints and patterns. Oh and did I forget to mention how totally cute this would look on your Instagram flat lays?

3. Wallet Cases

Want your phone to be fully protected while keeping your tech game sleek and corporate? A wallet phone case like this one from Bavin (Available in ZALORA) would be a good option! Made from high quality leather, wallet cases are stylish and functional, serving as storage for your identification cards, credit cards while keeping your device safe and protected.

4. Battery Cases

Tired of lugging around that heavy external battery pack but are in constant need of extra juice? A battery case is your best bet. A Battery case gives you that extra shot of much needed power while keeping your phone protected from hard drops and dents through its protective outershell.

Have other cool and functional cases to recommend? Share your suggestions in the comments section below!
